Our Features

We’ll Watch your Bids

Download our app and we’ll send you a notice if you’re bid has won. Bidding on a coveted art piece can be thrilling, but sometimes you don't have the time to track your bids.

Global Catalog

We’ve searched the entire world for the best art pieces and art sets man can find. Our growing database of 2,000 images of architecture, art, and design by 4,000 artists spans past history, modern, and contemporary works.

Save and Purchase Collections

Save art that you like for later viewing or purchase in a personal online collection. If so desired, you can purchase all items in your collection at once.

Authenticity

Everytime you purchase from us you are guaranteed to get authentic art or your money back, no questons asked.
